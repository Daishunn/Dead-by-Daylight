Main Gamemodes/Modifers

2v8

You can play as Killer or Survivor. Divided by 2 Killers and 8 Survivors

Survivors get new modified classes that come with unlockable skills.
Killers also get modified classes and can cage survivors

Chaos Shuffle

In this gamemode neither role can pick their perks and receive randomized set of perks once the match starts.

You dont have to own the perks

Lights Out

Drastically reduces the visibility and brightness of both roles. Focuses on stealth for both

Annual Gamemodes

Anniversary

Killers and Survivors get special event abilities in game and bloodpoints are increased. Special Offerings and Items are available in the bloodweb

Haunted By Daylight

Dead by Daylight's Halloween Event

Brings the Void Survivors and Killers can enter with Void energy. you can enter the void through filling a Void Rift with void energy

Scorching Summer BBQ

Dead by Daylight's Summer Event

Changes generators and hooks summer themed.

Bone Chill

Dead by Daylight's Winter Event that comes around December

Features Snowmen that both killers and survivors can enter and snow piles that provide snow skulls
