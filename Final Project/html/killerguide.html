Killer Guide

As Killer your objective is simple. Find and sacrafice all 4 survivors to the entity

Core Objectives

As killer you have some core objectives to reach your main goal

Find and Chase Survivors around the map

Chasing survivors helps secure you injuries and downs

Injure Survivors

Injuring survivors helps with getting downs and slowing down the pace of the game on sruvivors side

Hook Survivors

After getting a down hook a survivor u need to hook a survivor three times for them to be sacrificed.

Stop Generators from being repaired

Stopping generators from being repaired helps reduce gen pressure.

Breaking generators and bringing generator slow-down perks helps a lot

Sacrifice

Securing kills reduces the chances of survivors winning.

Each Killer is Unique

Though the Core objective for all killers is the same all killers have a different playstyle

All killers come with a unique power that changes the way that play the game

Killer playstyles can be grouped into categories

M1 Killers

M1 killers are killers whose gameplay focuses mainly on their basic attack for downs. Their power usually helps secure them M1 hits.

The Clown
Can slow survivors with his bottles and also speed himself and survivors
The Cenobite
Can chain survivors to leave the incapacitated

Ranged Killers

These Killers rely on projectiles as their main focus to get injuries and downs

The Huntress
Throws Hatchets
The Trickster
Hurls throwing knives in quick succession to build up stacks that leads to injuries and downs
The Artist
The Deathslinger
Has a harpoon can he can shoot and reel in survivors with

Stealth Killers

These Killers rely on being sneaky to stalk and ambush unsuspecting survivors

The Shape
Stalks survivors to swap to
The Wraith
Has the power of invisibility that he can enter and exit with the bell
The Ghost Face
Can creep around the map to stalk survivors and fill their stalk meter to leave them exposed for executions
The Pig
Walks around the map crouched to find and ambush survivors for downs.

Area Controls/Zone Killers

These Killers focus on placing "traps" around the map to zone suvivors and restrict movement and loops

The Trapper
Leaves traps around the map on window vaults and pallets to trap survivors
The Hag
Leaves Phantasm traps around the map that when set off by survivors she can teleport too.
The Skull Merchant
Has drones she places around to map to detect survivors

Mobility Killers

These Killers rely on high speed movement to cover ground fast to catch survivors

The Blight
His ability allows him to dash and rush around the map
The Spirit
can enter a phase state and allows her to traverse easily
The Nurse
her power lets her teleport through anything
The Hillbilly
The Hilbilly's chainsaw lets him traverse the map at high speeds
The Oni
Once the Onis power is full can enter Blood Fury and use Demon Dash to traverse the map
The Ghoul
has the ability to use his tentacles to jump and traverse the map freely

Hybrid Killers

These Killers are a combo and have a playstyle that fits multiple categories

The Onyro-Stealth and M1
A ghost that can teleport to TV's around the map while being undetectable unti; she manifests
The Legion- Mobility and M1
The legions power lets them enter
The Knight- M1 and Zone
Can use his guards to control survivors movements
The Dredge- Mobility, M1 and Stealth
Can teleport to lockers, but also has the ability to darken the entire map obstructing survivors vision.
The Xenomorph- Stealth, Ranged
The tail strike can be used for ranged and the Xenomorph can enter crawler mode which enables stealth
The Good Guy- Stealth and Mobility
Can enter Hidey-Ho mode to go undetectable and while in Hidey-HO can dash using slice and dice
The Demogorgon- Mobility and Zone
Can teleport to portals which he can use to zone survivors also has the power to lunge.

The best way to learn killers is to watch other play them. Their playstyles are all unique and complicated.
